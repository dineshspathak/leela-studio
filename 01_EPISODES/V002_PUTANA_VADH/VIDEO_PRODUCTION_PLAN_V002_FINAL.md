# 🎬 VIDEO PRODUCTION PLAN — V002: पूतना उद्धार (The Liberation of Putana)

This plan contains the 9 Google Flow video prompts and scene parameters required for Episode V002, transitioning the studio to a video-first workflow.

---

### **Scene 01: Kamsa's Paranoia**
*   **Google Flow Prompt**: `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, Kamsa pacing in a grand dark sandstone throne room, sweating face, paranoid expressions, low-key lighting with single red torchlight casting long shadows. Heavy breathing, camera slowly dollying back --ar 16:9`
*   **Scene Objective**: Establish Kamsa's extreme fear, paranoia, and the decree to kill Gokul's newborns.
*   **Character Dialogue**:
    *   *कंस*: "मथुरा के सैनिकों! गोकुल और उसके आसपास के हर उस शिशु का अंत कर दो... जिसने पिछले दस दिनों में जन्म लिया है!"
*   **Camera Movement**: Slow dolly back from extreme close-up on Kamsa's sweating face to reveal the dark empty throne room.
*   **Lighting**: Low-key lighting, deep shadows, single red torchlight casting flickering light on sandstone walls.
*   **Sound Design**: Footsteps echoing on stone floors, Kamsa's heavy breathing, distant wind howling outside.
*   **Music Cue**: Low-tension cello drone, deep dramatic string swell when Kamsa stops.
*   **Scene Duration**: 8 seconds
*   **Ending Frame Continuity**: Throne room fades into a dark, stormy night sky.

### **Scene 02: Putana Flying**
*   **Google Flow Prompt**: `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, giant green-grey demoness Putana flying fast through black storm clouds, red hair flying, fangs exposed, looking down with hollow eyes, lightning flashing --ar 16:9`
*   **Scene Objective**: Showcase Putana's terrifying form and speed as she flies toward Gokul.
*   **Character Dialogue**: None.
*   **Camera Movement**: Low angle looking up at the sky, tracking her flight path through the storm.
*   **Lighting**: Dark blue stormy sky, illuminated by periodic bright blue lightning flashes.
*   **Sound Design**: Heavy thunder rumbles, wind storm howling, deep heavy wingbeat sounds.
*   **Music Cue**: Rising dramatic brass and tense rhythmic percussion.
*   **Scene Duration**: 7 seconds
*   **Ending Frame Continuity**: Storm clouds break to reveal the golden rays of dawn over Gokul.

### **Scene 03: The Deceptive Beauty**
*   **Google Flow Prompt**: `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, beautiful woman in a saffron silk saree, jasmine flowers in hair, smiling gently, entering Nanda Bhavan's doorway in warm morning light --ar 16:9`
*   **Scene Objective**: Present Putana's beautiful disguise and the spell she casts on Nanda Bhavan.
*   **Character Dialogue**:
    *   *यशोदा*: "कौन है यह अपूर्व सुंदरी? जैसे साक्षात् लक्ष्मी वैकुंठ त्याग कर मेरे कान्हा को आशीष देने आई हों..."
*   **Camera Movement**: Slow pan left (orbit) tracking her saffron saree as she crosses the threshold.
*   **Lighting**: Warm morning sunlight filtering through banyan trees, soft oil lamp light inside Nanda Bhavan.
*   **Sound Design**: Birds chirping, distant cows lowing, wooden doors creaking open.
*   **Music Cue**: Soft sitar melody and slow bansuri flute loops.
*   **Scene Duration**: 9 seconds
*   **Ending Frame Continuity**: Putana stands by the wooden cradle, looking down.

### **Scene 04: The Palne Beat**
*   **Google Flow Prompt**: `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, close-up of cosmic blue baby Krishna closing his eyes in a wooden cradle, beautiful woman leaning over, casting a shadow --ar 16:9`
*   **Scene Objective**: Putana reaches the cradle, Krishna closes his eyes, building high suspense.
*   **Character Dialogue**:
    *   *पूतना (मन में)*: "कितना सुंदर बालक है... पर इसके प्राण ही कंस महाराज को शांति देंगे।"
*   **Camera Movement**: Slow push-in on the cradle, closing in on Baby Krishna's face.
*   **Lighting**: Shadow falling across the wooden cradle, contrast of golden baby skin and dark shadow.
*   **Sound Design**: Rhythmic creaking of the wooden cradle, soft baby breathing.
*   **Music Cue**: Single sustained, high-pitched temple bell ringing in absolute silence.
*   **Scene Duration**: 8 seconds
*   **Ending Frame Continuity**: Putana picks up the baby, bringing him to her chest.

### **Scene 05: The Sacred Breastfeeding**
*   **Google Flow Prompt**: `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, close-up of baby Krishna's tiny hands clutching her chest, golden veins of cosmic light pulsing and spreading, woman's face twisted in horror --ar 16:9`
*   **Scene Objective**: Putana feeds the baby poison; Krishna grabs her and sucks her lifeforce; Putana screams in agony.
*   **Character Dialogue**:
    *   *पूतना*: "अरे! यह कैसा बालक है? छोड़ो... मुझे छोड़ो! यह मेरा दूध नहीं, मेरे प्राण खींच रहा है! छोड़ो!"
*   **Camera Movement**: Slow zoom in on Putana's eyes widening in horror.
*   **Lighting**: Room shadows darken; bright golden light pulses from Krishna's chest.
*   **Sound Design**: Deep cosmic hum (vibrating), rapid double heartbeat sound effect rising.
*   **Music Cue**: Sanskrit cosmic choir chanting, loud epic percussion.
*   **Scene Duration**: 9 seconds
*   **Ending Frame Continuity**: Putana's skin starts cracking like dry clay under the golden light.
