# 🖼️ LEELA Studios — Thumbnail Plan: V002

This plan details the A/B testing thumbnail layouts for Episode V002 (*पूतना उद्धार*).

---

## Thumbnail Concept A (The Miracle — Focus on Awe)
*   **Visual Layout**: Close-up of baby Krishna (cosmic blue skin) looking up peacefully while golden divine cracks spread over Putana's chest in a dark prison/room. A glowing gold divine aura outlines his body.
*   **Text Overlay**: 
    *   *Text*: "जब विष अमृत बन गया!" (When Poison Became Nectar!)
    *   *Typography*: Bold yellow Sanskrit-style Hindi font with black outlines, top-third.
*   **Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art, close-up of cosmic blue baby Krishna sleeping, glowing golden cracks spreading from his hand across a dark chest, warm divine light rays, high contrast, vibrant colors --ar 16:9`

---

## Thumbnail Concept B (The Clash — Focus on Conflict)
*   **Visual Layout**: Split composition. Left: Kamsa's paranoid face pacing in his dark throne room. Right: The beautiful woman's disguise cracking open like clay, revealing the giant demoness with glowing eyes.
*   **Text Overlay**:
    *   *Text*: "पूतना का महाविनाश!" (The Destruction of Putana!)
    *   *Typography*: Bold red text with white outlines, bottom-third.
*   **Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art, split composition, left side Kamsa's paranoid face, right side beautiful woman's face cracking open to reveal giant dark demoness with red hair, high contrast, vibrant colors --ar 16:9`
