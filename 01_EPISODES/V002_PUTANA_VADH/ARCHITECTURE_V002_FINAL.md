# 📐 STORY ARCHITECTURE — V002: पूतना वध (The Liberation of Putana)

## Story Structure Blueprint

---

## Act-by-Act Breakdown

### Act 1 — The Shadow Over Gokul (0:00 — 2:00)
*   **Narrative Goal**: Establish the looming threat. Putana represents Kamsa's desperate reach into the safe haven of Gokul.
*   **Beats**:
    *   **Beat 1 (Cold Open / Hook)**: Visual of a dark, giant shadow flying across the moonlit sky, heading towards a peaceful village.
    *   **Beat 2**: Narration on Kamsa's decree—slay every newborn. The paranoia reaches Gokul.
*   **Pacing**: Fast, tense.
*   **Emotional Intensity**: Level 4 (Major Emotional Moment).

### Act 2 — The Beautiful Deception (2:00 — 4:30)
*   **Narrative Goal**: Create contrast. Show the beauty of Putana's disguise and the vulnerability of Gokul's innocence.
*   **Beats**:
    *   **Beat 1**: Putana transforms into a beautiful woman. Saffron saree, jasmine flowers, glowing face.
    *   **Beat 2**: She walks through Gokul streets. Gopis think she is Lakshmi herself.
    *   **Beat 3**: Entering Nanda Bhavan. Yashoda and Rohini watch her approach the cradle, mesmerized, unable to move due to her illusionary motherly charm.
*   **Pacing**: Slow, flowing, hypnotic.
*   **Emotional Intensity**: Level 2 (Conversation/Atmosphere).

### Act 3 — The Divine Poison (4:30 — 7:00)
*   **Narrative Goal**: The confrontation. The peak theological mystery.
*   **Beats**:
    *   **Beat 1**: Putana picks up baby Krishna. The Kalkuta poison is smeared on her breast.
    *   **Beat 2**: Krishna closes his eyes. Narration explores the mystery: why did he close his eyes?
    *   **Beat 3**: The suck. Krishna grips her breast. He draws the poison, but does not stop—he begins drawing her very life force (*prana*).
*   **Pacing**: Tense, slow, suspended time.
*   **Emotional Intensity**: Level 5 (Divine Miracle).

### Act 4 — The Giant Fall (7:00 — 8:30)
*   **Narrative Goal**: The release of tension. The physical destruction of the demoness.
*   **Beats**:
    *   **Beat 1**: Putana screams. Her illusion shatters. The beautiful skin cracks, revealing her massive dark demoness form.
    *   **Beat 2**: She runs out of the house. Her miles-long body falls dead outside Gokul, crushing the forest trees.
    *   **Beat 3**: Gopis run out and rescue baby Krishna, who is playing and laughing on her chest.
*   **Pacing**: Explosive, fast cuts.
*   **Emotional Intensity**: Level 4 (Major Emotional Moment).

### Act 5 — The Sandalwood Smoke (8:30 — 10:00)
*   **Narrative Goal**: Resolution and theological landing. The concept of absolute redemption.
*   **Beats**:
    *   **Beat 1**: The cremation. Gokul residents chop her body. When they burn it, the smoke smells of pure sandalwood.
    *   **Beat 2 (The Lesson)**: If someone who came to murder God with poison gets liberated as a mother, what happens to those who approach him with love?
*   **Pacing**: Soothing, slow, deep.
*   **Emotional Intensity**: Level 3 (Emotion/Wisdom).
