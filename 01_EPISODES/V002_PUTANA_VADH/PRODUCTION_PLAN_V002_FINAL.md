# 🎬 LEELA Studios — Production Plan: V002

**Episode Title**: *पूतना उद्धार — जब विष भी अमृत बन गया* (The Liberation of Putana)  
**Status**: Pre-Production (Awaiting Approval)

---

## 1. Episode Objective
To retell the sacred narrative of baby Krishna defeating and liberating the demoness Putana in Gokul. The central goal is to evoke the emotion of **AWE (विस्मय)** and **DEVOTION (भक्ति)**, illustrating the theological lesson of divine redemption: even those who approach the Lord with poison are purified and granted liberation by his touch.

---

## 2. Estimated Runtime
*   **Total Runtime**: **10:52** (including pre-built intro/outro animations)
*   **Word Count**: ~1,200 words of pure Hindi narration (paced at 1.1x / ~130 words per minute).
