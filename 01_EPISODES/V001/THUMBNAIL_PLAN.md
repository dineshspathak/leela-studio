# 🖼️ LEELA Studios — Thumbnail Plan: V001

To maximize click-through rate (CTR), V001 utilizes a dual-concept A/B testing strategy. Both layouts focus on high visual contrast, clear human faces (or silhouettes), and bold text overlays designed for mobile screens.

---

## Thumbnail Concept A (The Miracle — Focus on Awe)

*   **Visual Layout**: Deep blue-black roaring Yamuna River parting symmetrically under a full moon. In the center, a strong silhouette of Vasudeva wading through, holding baby Krishna high. Heavy rain streaks falling, protected by Sheshnag's massive hoods visible in the storm.
*   **Key Colors**: Dark Blue (`#1e3a8a`), Metallic Gold (`#d97706`), Silver-White (`#f3f4f6`).
*   **Text Overlay**: 
    *   *Text*: "जिस रात नदी भी झुक गई!" (The Night the River Bowed!)
    *   *Typography*: Large, bold, Sanskrit-style Hindi font with a metallic gold texture and a thick black drop shadow for readability. Placed in the upper third.
*   **Prompt for Generation**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art, deep blue and gold color scheme, roaring Yamuna river parting symmetrically, full moon sky, silhouette of a man carrying baby, five-headed serpent hood above, high contrast, vibrant colors, epic composition, looking directly at the camera, cosmic blue and gold gradient background, sharp focus, 1280x720 composition --ar 16:9`

---

## Thumbnail Concept B (The Clash — Focus on Conflict/Fear)

*   **Visual Layout**: Split-screen composition. On the left: close-up of Kamsa's paranoid, angry face looking down with a drawn sword. On the right: Devaki cowering in the dark prison cell, holding a glowing golden baby Krishna.
*   **Key Colors**: Deep Crimson Red (`#991b1b`), Dark Grey/Iron (`#374151`), Luminous Gold (`#f59e0b`).
*   **Text Overlay**:
    *   *Text*: "ज़ंजीरें खुद टूट गईं!" (The Chains Broke Themselves!)
    *   *Typography*: Bold, crimson red text with white outlines and a heavy black shadow. Placed in the bottom third.
*   **Prompt for Generation**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art, split composition, left side Kamsa's angry face holding sword, right side glowing golden baby in dark prison cell, high contrast, vibrant colors, epic composition, cosmic blue and gold gradient background, sharp focus, 1280x720 composition --ar 16:9`

---

## A/B Testing Strategy
1.  **Launch**: Deploy **Concept A** immediately upon upload.
2.  **Evaluation**: Track CTR after 24 hours. If CTR is < 6%, switch to **Concept B**.
3.  **Final Polish**: Ensure text occupies no more than 20% of the total thumbnail canvas.
