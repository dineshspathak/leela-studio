# 🎨 LEELA Studios — Visual Style Validation Package: V001

This package defines the visual baseline for Episode V001 and all future LEELA Studios productions. The following 5 keyframes establish the lighting, composition, color palette, and character design parameters for the entire series.

---

## 1. Keyframe 01: The Prison Cell

*   **Location ID**: `LOC-PRISON-001`
*   **Character ID**: `CHAR-DEVAKI-001` (in background silhouette)
*   **Final Production Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, photorealistic divine painting, 4K resolution, deep blue and slate-grey color scheme with single warm oil lamp light source, inside a cold windowless Mathura prison cell, damp sandstone walls catching humidity, heavy iron chains hanging from walls, a flickering oil lamp on a small stone ledge casting warm orange highlights and dramatic chiaroscuro shadows, Devaki sitting in deep shadow in the background. --ar 16:9`
*   **Negative Prompt**:
    `modern light sources, concrete walls, plastic textures, bright ambient lighting, generic fantasy art style, 3D render look, low resolution, multiple lamps, neon color.`
*   **Camera**: Wide shot, low angle, locked frame.
*   **Lens**: 28mm wide-angle lens (to emphasize claustrophobia and the weight of the stone walls).
*   **Lighting**: Single-point dramatic chiaroscuro. Warm key-light (`#ea580c` flame glow) contrasting deep cool blue shadow fills (`#1e293b`).
*   **Composition**: Golden triangle rule. The flickering lamp is at the lower-right intersection; Devaki's silhouette is at the upper-left intersection.
*   **Color Palette**: Midnight Blue (`#0a0f1d`), Slate Grey (`#475569`), Warm Amber (`#d97706`).
*   **Continuity Notes**: The texture of the sandstone walls and the rust pattern on the iron chains must match exactly in subsequent prison shots (e.g., Scene 07 and Scene 11).

## 2. Keyframe 02: The Wedding Scene

*   **Location ID**: `LOC-WEDDING-001`
*   **Character IDs**: `CHAR-DEVAKI-001` (bride), `CHAR-VASUDEVA-001` (groom), `CHAR-KAMSA-001` (driver)
*   **Final Production Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, photorealistic divine painting, 4K resolution, vibrant warm color scheme, a grand wedding chariot procession moving through ancient Mathura streets, marigold garlands draped over sandstone arches, Devaki as a beautiful bride and Vasudeva as a handsome groom sitting side-by-side inside the gold-gilded chariot, Kamsa driving in front with a proud smile, warm golden hour sun filtering through dust, falling flower petals. --ar 16:9`
*   **Negative Prompt**:
    `modern clothes, modern vehicles, dark moody lighting, desaturated colors, cartoon style, anime, modern buildings, telephone poles.`
*   **Camera**: Mid-wide tracking shot, eye level.
*   **Lens**: 50mm portrait lens (to balance character detail with the scale of the procession).
*   **Lighting**: Backlit golden hour sunshine (`#f59e0b` rim lighting) with warm, diffused front fill.
*   **Composition**: Rule of Thirds. Kamsa occupies the left third; the couple occupies the right third; leading lines of the chariot guide the eye horizontally.
*   **Color Palette**: Marigold Yellow (`#fbbf24`), Royal Maroon (`#7f1d1d`), Sandstone Gold (`#d97706`), Off-White (`#f8fafc`).
*   **Continuity Notes**: Devaki's wedding skin tone must match her prison appearance (just healthier and dressed in royal silk). Kamsa's facial structure and hair tie must be identical to his palace scenes.

## 3. Keyframe 03: Vishnu Appears

*   **Location ID**: `LOC-PRISON-001`
*   **Character ID**: `CHAR-VISHNU-001`
*   **Final Production Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, photorealistic divine painting, 4K resolution, cosmic blue and luminous gold color palette, four-armed Lord Vishnu appearing inside a dark stone prison cell, body radiating an intense white-gold divine aura, holding a conch shell, disc, mace, and lotus, wearing a golden crown with a peacock feather, Kaustubha gem glowing on his chest, yellow silk garments blowing in divine wind. --ar 16:9`
*   **Negative Prompt**:
    `scary expression, dark shadow on face, cartoon, flat colors, modern clothes, extra limbs other than the four arms, generic angel wings.`
*   **Camera**: Low-angle medium-wide shot, centered framing.
*   **Lens**: 85mm cinematic lens (to compress the background stone walls and draw focus entirely to the divine scale).
*   **Lighting**: Internal volumetric light source. Luminous gold rays (`#fef08a`) emanating outwards from Vishnu's body, completely overpowering the prison darkness.
*   **Composition**: Centered symmetry. Reserved strictly for Level 5 Divine Miracles. Lord Vishnu is dead-center, drawing absolute focus and creating a feeling of stability and awe.
*   **Color Palette**: Cosmic Blue (`#1d4ed8`), Radiant Gold (`#fbbf24`), Pure White (`#ffffff`), Saffron (`#f97316`).
*   **Continuity Notes**: The skin color of Lord Vishnu must match the deep cosmic blue standard (`#1d4ed8`). This exact shade must be carried over to Baby Krishna, Child Krishna, and King Krishna.

## 4. Keyframe 04: Yamuna Crossing

*   **Location ID**: `LOC-RIVER-001`
*   **Character IDs**: `CHAR-VASUDEVA-001`, `CHAR-KRISHNA-BABY-001`, `CHAR-SHESHNAG-001`
*   **Final Production Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, photorealistic divine painting, 4K resolution, deep blue-black storm palette, Vasudeva wading waist-deep through the roaring monsoon Yamuna river at night, holding baby Krishna high above his head, a massive five-headed dark serpent Sheshnag spreading its hoods protectively above them like an umbrella, torrential rain falling, lightning illuminating white foam and waves. --ar 16:9`
*   **Negative Prompt**:
    `bright daylight, calm water, blue sky, modern boats, bridge in background, baby crying, cartoon style, flat colors.`
*   **Camera**: Extreme low-angle tracking shot, wide field of view.
*   **Lens**: 35mm lens (capturing the violent scale of the river waves and the towering height of Sheshnag's hoods).
*   **Lighting**: High-contrast dramatic key-lighting triggered by lightning flashes (`#e2e8f0` cold highlights) contrasting with dark, foaming water shadows.
*   **Composition**: Vertical hierarchy. Sheshnag's hoods dominate the upper third; baby Krishna is held high in the center third; Vasudeva's body wades in the bottom third.
*   **Color Palette**: Ink Black (`#030712`), Storm Blue (`#1e3a8a`), Foaming White (`#f9fafb`), Amber eyes (`#d97706` for the serpent).
*   **Continuity Notes**: Vasudeva's wet hair, clingy white dhoti, and shackle marks must be consistent with the prison escape scenes. The baby's skin color must match Vishnu's cosmic blue.

## 5. Keyframe 05: Baby Krishna Beside Yashoda

*   **Location ID**: `LOC-YASHODA-HOUSE-001`
*   **Character IDs**: `CHAR-KRISHNA-BABY-001`, `CHAR-YASHODA-001`
*   **Final Production Prompt**:
    `Cinematic Indian mythological art style, photorealistic divine painting, 4K resolution, soft warm domestic color palette, extreme close-up, cosmic blue newborn baby Krishna sleeping peacefully on a woven straw mat inside Nanda's Gokul home, a single peacock feather tucked behind his ear, sleeping mother Yashoda visible in soft blur in the background, soft morning window light. --ar 16:9`
*   **Negative Prompt**:
    `crying baby, modern nursery, crib, plastic toys, bright studio lighting, cartoon, oversaturated primary colors.`
*   **Camera**: Extreme close-up (ECU), shallow depth of field.
*   **Lens**: 100mm macro lens (to create maximum intimacy, focusing on the fine textures of the baby's skin and the straw mat).
*   **Lighting**: Diffused side-lighting from a dawn window. Soft golden rays (`#fef08a` at 40% intensity) creating gentle transitions on the face.
*   **Composition**: Shallow depth-of-field rule. The baby's face is in sharp focus in the left foreground; Yashoda's peaceful face is softly blurred in the right background.
*   **Color Palette**: Cosmic Blue (`#1e3a8a`), Straw Yellow (`#fef08a`), Earth Terracotta (`#c2410c`), Soft Red (`#b91c1c`).
*   **Continuity Notes**: The baby's single peacock feather must match the exact placement and size from the transformation scene (Scene 10) to maintain visual logic.
