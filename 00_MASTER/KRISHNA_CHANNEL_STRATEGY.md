## 7. THUMBNAIL STRATEGY

### Thumbnail Formula (3-Element Rule):
Every thumbnail MUST have:
1. **Stunning Krishna image** (AI-generated, divine lighting, blue skin)
2. **Bold Hindi text** (2-4 words maximum, yellow/white on dark)
3. **Emotion trigger** (curiosity, wonder, shock, devotion)

### Color Palette:
- **Background:** Deep blue / dark purple / cosmic black
- **Krishna:** Vibrant blue skin, golden crown, yellow dhoti
- **Text:** Bright yellow (₹ gold) or pure white
- **Accent:** Red (urgency), Green (peace), Orange (divine energy)

### Text Style Rules:
- Font: Bold Devanagari or thick Hindi fonts
- Maximum 4 words on thumbnail
- Add "?" for curiosity-driven videos
- Use numbers when possible ("7 Rahasya", "3 Chamatkar")

### Thumbnail Templates by Category:
| Category | Style | Example Text |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Stories | Krishna in action scene | "कृष्ण का क्रोध" |
| Life Lessons | Krishna speaking pose | "ये गलती मत करना" |
| Mysteries | Cosmic/mystical visuals | "कृष्ण के 7 रहस्य" |
| Love Stories | Radha-Krishna | "राधा की विरह" |
| Mahabharat | Battle/dramatic scenes | "अर्जुन का संदेह" |

### A/B Testing Strategy:
- Create 2 thumbnails for every video
- Test for 48 hours, keep the winner
- Track CTR — aim for 8%+ (devotional niche average is 5-6%)

## 8. VIDEO STYLE

### Visual Structure (Every Video):
```
0:00 - 0:15  → HOOK (dramatic question or statement)
0:15 - 0:45  → Context setting (who, where, when)
0:45 - 8:00  → Main story (with dramatic peaks)
8:00 - 9:30  → Life lesson / modern connection
9:30 - 10:00 → CTA (like, subscribe, next video tease)
```

### Visual Elements:
- **AI-generated Krishna artwork** (using Midjourney/DALL-E/Flux style)
- **Slow zoom/pan effects** on images (Ken Burns effect)
- **Particle effects** — divine lights, golden sparkles
- **Color grading** — warm golden tones for Vrindavan, cool blue for cosmic scenes
- **Transition style** — soft dissolves (never hard cuts for devotional)

### Audio Layering:
1. **Voice** — Clear female Hindi narration (Sarvam AI - Priya/Neha)
2. **Background music** — Soft flute (bansuri) + tabla at low volume
3. **Sound effects** — Temple bells, conch shell, nature sounds at story points

### Mood Board:
- Think: "Netflix documentary meets temple experience"
- Not: Slideshow of Google images (what competitors do)
- Quality reference: Kurzgesagt-style quality but for Indian mythology
