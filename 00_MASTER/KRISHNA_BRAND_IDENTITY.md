## 4. TONE OF VOICE

### Primary Tone: **Reverent Wonder**

Like a storyteller who is themselves amazed by the story they're telling.

### Voice Guidelines:

| Situation | Tone | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Opening hook | Curiosity + Drama | "उस रात, जब पूरी पृथ्वी अंधकार में डूबी थी..." |
| Story narration | Flowing + Immersive | "कृष्ण ने धीरे से बांसुरी उठाई, और समय जैसे ठहर गया" |

| Life lessons | Warm + Wise | "और यही बात हमें सिखाती है कि..." |

### Language Style:
- **Hindi:** Pure, poetic, literary (शुद्ध हिंदी) — NOT Hinglish for this channel
- **Register:** Elevated but accessible (like a premium documentary narrator)
- **Sentence rhythm:** Long flowing sentences for stories, short punchy for impact
- **Signature phrases:**
  - "इस कथा को सुनिए..." (Listen to this tale...)
  - "और तब हुआ वो, जो किसी ने सोचा नहीं था..." (And then happened what no one expected...)

| Channel name/Logo | **Custom lettering** | — | Unique identity (see Logo section) |

### Typography Rules:
1. **Maximum 4-5 words on any thumbnail** — less is more
2. **Hindi text always in Devanagari script** — never Romanized Hindi
3. **Font size hierarchy:** Title (72pt) → Subtitle (48pt) → Body (24pt)
4. **Never use Comic Sans, Papyrus, or decorative fonts** — kills premium feel
5. **Letter spacing:** Slightly expanded (+2%) for elegance
6. **Text always has contrast layer** — dark shadow or outline for readability

### Thumbnail Text Style:
- Color: Sacred Gold (#f59e0b) or Moonlight White (#f8fafc)
- Background: Cosmic Black or Krishna Blue gradient
- Effect: Subtle golden glow/outer light behind text
- Position: Bottom-third or center (never top — gets cropped on mobile)

## 11. THUMBNAIL STYLE GUIDE

### The LEELA Thumbnail Formula — "3 Layers of Attraction"

**Layer 1 — The Visual (70% of thumbnail space):**
- Stunning AI-generated Krishna artwork
- One clear subject/scene (not cluttered)
- Dramatic lighting (divine glow, rim light, volumetric fog)
- Cinematic composition (rule of thirds)

**Layer 2 — The Text (20% of thumbnail space):**
- Maximum 3-5 words in bold Devanagari
- Positioned in lower-third or as overlay
- Gold text on dark area OR white text with dark shadow
- Font: Noto Serif Devanagari ExtraBold

**Layer 3 — The Emotion (10% — subtle cues):**
- Color temperature tells the mood (warm=love, cool=mystery, red=danger)
- Character expressions (wonder, fear, devotion, anger)
- Symbolic elements (peacock feather, flute, chakra, lotus)

### Thumbnail Templates (5 Signature Styles):

**Style A — "The Reveal" (Mystery/Curiosity):**
- Dark background, single beam of light on Krishna
- Text: Question format ("कृष्ण ने ऐसा क्यों किया?")
- Mood: Dark, dramatic, makes you NEED to know

**Style B — "The Epic" (Mahabharat/War):**
- Wide cinematic landscape, armies or cosmic scale
- Krishna in powerful pose (Vishwaroop, chariot, commanding)
- Text: Bold statement, minimal words
- Mood: Grand, awe-inspiring, goosebumps

**Style C — "The Tender" (Love/Emotion):**
- Soft lighting, intimate framing
- Radha-Krishna or emotional moment
- Warm golden tones, slightly soft focus
- Text: Emotional, poetic
- Mood: Makes you feel love or tears

**Style D — "The Wisdom" (Gita/Teachings):**
- Krishna speaking pose, divine aura
- Sunrise/dawn colors (amber, gold, white)
- Clean, minimal, space for text
- Text: Teaching or powerful quote
- Mood: Peaceful, authoritative

**Style E — "The Miracle" (Supernatural):**
- Cosmic visuals, impossible scales
- Bright divine energy, particle effects
- Krishna in supernatural act
- Text: Shock/wonder trigger
- Mood: Awe, "how is this possible?"

3. **Avoid pure white backgrounds** — they disappear on YouTube's white UI
4. **Face/eyes trigger clicks** — Krishna's face looking at viewer = powerful

## 20. BRAND RULES — The LEELA Commandments

### Every future video MUST follow these 15 rules:

**Visual Rules:**
1. ✅ **The LEELA Intro plays at 0:00** — same 5-second intro, every video, always
2. ✅ **Color grading is consistent** — deep blue/gold tone, never flat or desaturated
3. ✅ **Logo watermark** appears bottom-right (small, semi-transparent "LEELA" text)
4. ✅ **Golden border/frame** subtly appears in at least one scene transition
5. ✅ **Thumbnail follows one of the 5 style templates** — never random or off-brand

**Audio Rules:**
6. ✅ **Same narrator voice** in every video (Sarvam AI — choose ONE voice, keep forever)
7. ✅ **Background score starts within first 3 seconds** (bansuri or ambient divine music)
8. ✅ **Silence used intentionally** for emotional peaks (1-2 sec pause = goosebumps)
9. ✅ **No music louder than the narrator** — voice is king, music is atmosphere

**Content Rules:**
10. ✅ **Hook in first 8 seconds** — open with drama, mystery, or an impossible question
11. ✅ **Every story ends with a life lesson** — never just tell a story without modern relevance
12. ✅ **Scripturally accurate** — always cite the source (Bhagavat Purana, Mahabharat, etc.)
13. ✅ **No politics, no controversy** — the channel is a sacred space, keep it pure
14. ✅ **Signature closing line:** "जय श्री कृष्ण 🙏" (spoken by narrator at the end)

**Growth Rules:**
15. ✅ **End every video with a tease for the next** — "अगली कहानी में, कृष्ण ने किया कुछ ऐसा..."
