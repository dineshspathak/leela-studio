# 🎨 STORYBOARD — V002: पूतना उद्धार (The Liberation of Putana)

This storyboard maps the visual framing, camera instructions, CapCut effects, and audio tracks for all scenes in Episode V002, complying with the **LEELA Cinematic Rule** and **Visual Style Bible**.

---

## Storyboard List

### **Scene 01: Intro Animation (0:00 — 0:05)**
*   **Visual**: Pre-built gold logo animation.
*   **Camera**: None (built-in).
*   **Overlay**: Golden trail.
*   **Audio**: Bansuri note rising → Om bell.

### **Scene 02: Shadow Over Gokul (0:05 — 0:20)**
*   **Visual**: Wide shot of moonlit Gokul fields. A massive, dark clawed shadow sweeping over the landscape, blocking the moon for a second.
*   **Camera**: Slow Zoom Out.
*   **Overlay**: Drifting low fog, moonlit sky.
*   **Audio**: Low wind howl, deep heavy wingbeat sound.

### **Scene 03: Kamsa's Decree (0:20 — 0:40)**
*   **Visual**: Close-up of Kamsa in his dark palace corridor, pacing, sword in hand, looking paranoid and wild-eyed.
*   **Camera**: Slow Push-In.
*   **Overlay**: Flickering torch light overlay.
*   **Audio**: Tense, low string drone, Kamsa's footsteps echoing.

### **Scene 04: Putana Flying (0:40 — 1:05)**
*   **Visual**: Giant, terrifying dark grey-green demoness Putana flying through monsoon storm clouds, red hair flying, fangs exposed.
*   **Camera**: Pan Right (following motion).
*   **Overlay**: Storm rain overlay, dark cloud drift.
*   **Audio**: Thunder rumble, dark wind rise.

### **Scene 05: Transformation (1:05 — 1:30)**
*   **Visual**: A bright golden flash. When it clears, a beautiful woman stands on the Gokul forest path, dressed in saffron silk.
*   **Camera**: Locked Frame.
*   **Overlay**: Golden divine flare transition.
*   **Audio**: Chime/swoosh, transitioning to soft shehnai.

### **Scene 06: Walking Gokul (1:30 — 2:00)**
*   **Visual**: Putana in her beautiful disguise walking through Gokul village streets, Gopis watching with folded hands in admiration.
*   **Camera**: Slow Left Pan (Orbit).
*   **Overlay**: Volumetric morning light shafts.
*   **Audio**: Sitar and bansuri theme.

### **Scene 07: Entering Nanda Bhavan (2:00 — 2:35)**
*   **Visual**: Putana crossing the threshold of Nanda Bhavan, a warm domestic interior with pots and copper vessels.
*   **Camera**: Tilt Down (following her entry).
*   **Overlay**: Soft oil lamp flicker.
*   **Audio**: Soft wind chimes.

### **Scene 08: Yashoda Spellbound (2:35 — 3:05)**
*   **Visual**: Close-up of Yashoda, eyes glazed and unfocused, spellbound by the motherly aura of the disguised demoness, standing completely still.
*   **Camera**: Locked Frame.
*   **Overlay**: Dark vignette (+30% focus on eyes).
*   **Audio**: Single sustained drone, absolute silence.

### **Scene 09: Putana Approaching Cradle (3:05 — 3:30)**
*   **Visual**: The beautiful woman leaning over the wooden cradle containing baby Krishna, a shadow falling over the baby's face.
*   **Camera**: Slow Push-In.
*   **Overlay**: Oil lamp flicker.
*   **Audio**: Drone continues, heartbeat sound effect starts (slow).

### **Scene 10: Snatches Baby & Krishna Closes Eyes (3:30 — 4:10)**
*   **Visual**: Putana lifting baby Krishna from the cradle. Extreme close-up of cosmic blue baby Krishna closing his eyes tightly.
*   **Camera**: Extreme Close-up (ECU) of baby's closed eyes.
*   **Overlay**: Soft golden catchlight on baby's face.
*   **Audio**: Heartbeat stops. Absolute silence.

### **Scene 11: Offers Poison Breast (4:10 — 4:40)**
*   **Visual**: Putana holding Krishna, offering her breast. A dark, oily substance (poison) visible on her skin.
*   **Camera**: Medium shot.
*   **Lens**: 85mm lens.
*   **Overlay**: Low-light vignette.
*   **Audio**: Muffled baby breathing.

### **Scene 12: Krishna Sucks Life (4:40 — 5:25)**
*   **Visual**: Baby Krishna's tiny hands clutching her breast. Golden cracks of divine light begin pulsing from the baby's hands into her chest. Putana's expression changes to pure shock and horror.
*   **Camera**: Slow Zoom In.
*   **Overlay**: Luminous gold pulse overlay.
*   **Audio**: Deep vibrating cosmic hum rising in volume.

### **Scene 13: Putana Screams (5:25 — 6:00)**
*   **Visual**: Close-up of Putana's face screaming in agony, her beautiful face skin beginning to crack open like dry clay.
*   **Camera**: Locked Frame.
*   **Overlay**: Screen shake effect.
*   **Audio**: High-pitch shattering sound, echoing scream.

### **Scene 14: Demoness Bursts Out (6:00 — 6:35)**
*   **Visual**: Her beautiful disguise shatters completely. Her original giant grey-green demoness form with red hair and fangs stands towering.
*   **Camera**: Tilt Up to show scale.
*   **Overlay**: Dust explosion, wind particles.
*   **Audio**: Giant roar, wooden beams cracking.

### **Scene 15: Runs and Falls (6:35 — 7:15)**
*   **Visual**: Putana running out of Nanda Bhavan and collapsing outside the village, her multi-mile long body crashing onto trees.
*   **Camera**: Drone wide shot.
*   **Overlay**: Low fog, rising dust clouds.
*   **Audio**: Massive tree snap/collapse SFX, heavy ground thud.

### **Scene 16: Rescuing Krishna (7:15 — 8:00)**
*   **Visual**: Gokul residents gathered at the giant corpse. Baby Krishna sitting on her chest, laughing, playing with his feet.
*   **Camera**: Low-angle medium-shot.
*   **Overlay**: Morning sunrays.
*   **Audio**: Baby giggle, Gopis gasping.

### **Scene 17: Purification Ritual (8:00 — 8:35)**
*   **Visual**: Gopis bathing baby Krishna, Rohini waving a cow's tail over him, and Yashoda applying sacred dust to his forehead.
*   **Camera**: Slow Pull-Out.
*   **Overlay**: Volumetric warm light.
*   **Audio**: Temple chanting/bell loops (low volume).

### **Scene 18: Funeral Pyre (8:35 — 9:30)**
*   **Visual**: Putana's body pieces burning on a massive funeral pyre. Beautiful, golden-white smoke rising, drifting gently over Gokul houses.
*   **Camera**: Slow Left Pan.
*   **Overlay**: Golden particles, warm fire sparks.
*   **Audio**: Warm crackling fire, soft bansuri flute theme starts.

### **Scene 19: The Lesson (9:30 — 10:10)**
*   **Visual**: Peaceful Vrindavan forest at sunset, golden rays filtering through banyan trees, cows returning home.
*   **Camera**: Slow Zoom Out.
*   **Overlay**: Volumetric god rays.
*   **Audio**: Flute theme peaks, soothing and warm.

### **Scene 20: Whirlwind Tease (10:10 — 10:40)**
*   **Visual**: Gokul outskirts. A sudden violent dust storm (whirlwind) spinning rapidly, sucking up leaves and dust. A pair of glowing red eyes in the center.
*   **Camera**: Fast Push-In.
*   **Overlay**: Heavy dust whirlwind overlay.
*   **Audio**: Flute cuts. Rushing wind howling sound.

### **Scene 21: Outro (10:40 — 10:52)**
*   **Visual**: Black screen → LEELA logo fades.
*   **Camera**: None.
*   **Overlay**: Golden particles.
*   **Audio**: Bansuri note, Om bell.
